About Sri Gokulam Goshala

A Sacred Journey of Compassion, Protection, and Divine Service

Our Sacred Foundation

Sri Gokulam Goshala stands as a beacon of hope for cows across Telangana. Founded in 2025, our trust embodies the ancient Indian principles of Ahimsa (non-violence), Karuna (compassion), and Gau Seva (cow service).

In the sacred texts of Hinduism, cows are revered as Gau Mata (Mother Cow) - divine beings symbolizing gentleness, generosity, abundance, and purity. The cow is considered the earthly embodiment of all gods and goddesses.

Our trust was born from the urgent realization that these sacred beings, who have served humanity selflessly for millennia, are now facing unprecedented challenges in our rapidly modernizing world.

Meet Our Sacred Founders

Shri Sundilla Banaiah

Founder & President

A dedicated retired officer from Singerani Collieries, Shri Sundilla Banaiah's journey towards cow protection began during his childhood. In his young age he used to help his father in the farm fields. Back in the day his father owned many cows and bulls which were used in the fields. Compassionate motherly natured cows were a part of their life.

However, in his adulthood due to his professional obligations he could not continue the gorsewa as he did in his childhood. He witnessed the heartbreaking sight of cows on roadsides in urban localities, their eyes filled with pain and betrayal from the very humans they had served.

After retirement in 2015, instead of settling into a comfortable life, he made a life-changing decision. Using his entire provident fund and pension savings, he leased a small piece of land and purchased the first batch of 12 cows.

Since 2015 when the service was started without any documentation and now after the number of cows reached 158* unable to bear the crisis, had gone for registration of trust in April 2025, under the name of "SRI GOKULAM GOSHALA TRUST". The trust is formed so that many kind-hearted people who have love and affection towards cows can become part of this noble cause by the way they can support within their limits.

Smt. Sundilla Susheela

Co-Founder & Treasurer

A compassionate homemaker and devoted mother, Smt. Sundilla Susheela comes from agricultural background family. She also has a childhood growing among many cows. She being a devoted wife accompanied Shri Sundilla Banaiah in the goseva.

Her Key Contributions:

  • Nutrition Management: Plans balanced diet for each cow based on age, health condition, and nutritional needs
  • Health Monitoring: Maintains detailed health records and identifies early signs of illness
  • Volunteer Coordination: Trains and manages volunteers in proper animal care techniques
  • Financial Management: Maintains transparent accounts and donor communications
  • Emergency Response: Coordinates rescue operations and emergency medical care

Our Mission & Current Crisis

To provide lifelong sanctuary to elderly cows while creating awareness about the importance of cow protection in maintaining ecological balance, cultural values, and spiritual well-being of society.

Current Crisis

The major crisis we face today is the lack of proper shelter. Presently the cows are sheltered at a leased small piece of land.

  • During rainy season the space congestion causing swamp in the land making Cows more prone to infections and diseases
  • During the summer the excessive heat causing health issues
  • Being stagnant in a congested place for a long time is making it difficult for the cows to conceive and causing mastitis infection

Expansion Plans

Building a Sustainable Future for Sacred Cows

Our Vision for Growth:

  • Purchase 20 acres of permanent land: Secure a forever home for our sacred cows
  • Build modern cattle shelters for 500+ cows: Weather-protected spacious facilities with proper ventilation
  • Establish on-site veterinary hospital: Complete medical care facility with qualified veterinarians
  • Create organic farming unit using cow dung: Generate sustainable income through organic fertilizer production
  • Set up goseva training center: Educate youth and volunteers about scientific cow care
  • Develop sustainable income sources: Dairy products, biogas, organic farming to support goshala operations

Why This Matters: With your support, we can transform Sri Gokulam Goshala from a temporary shelter into a permanent sanctuary that will serve thousands of cows for generations to come.
