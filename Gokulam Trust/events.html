Sacred Festivals We Celebrate

Experience the divine joy of traditional cow worship festivals and spiritual celebrations

Gopashtami

Kartik Shukla Ashtami

The most auspicious day for cow worship, celebrating Lord Krishna's divine connection with cows. Our sacred mothers are worshipped with special rituals and offerings.

Activities:

  • Special cow worship rituals
  • Traditional decorations
  • Devotional singing and prayers
  • Special feeding for all cows

Makar Sankranthi

January 14th Annual

Harvest festival honoring cows and bulls for their contribution to agriculture. Cows are painted with vibrant colors and adorned with traditional ornaments.

Activities:

  • Colorful cow decorations
  • Traditional harvest rituals
  • Community feast
  • Kite flying celebration

Janmashtami

Krishna Janma Celebration

Celebrating Lord Krishna's birth with midnight prayers and cow blessings. The entire goshala is illuminated with diyas and decorated with flowers.

Activities:

  • Midnight prayers and kirtans
  • Dahi Handi celebration
  • Special cow blessings
  • Traditional dance performances

Holi Celebration

Festival of Colors

Celebrating the divine play of colors with our sacred mothers. Natural organic colors are gently applied to cows as part of the festivities.

Activities:

  • Natural color celebration
  • Traditional music and dance
  • Community feast
  • Cow blessing ceremony

Akshaya Tritiya

Auspicious Beginning

The golden day for new beginnings and prosperity. Cows are worshipped for bringing abundance and good fortune to all devotees.

Activities:

  • Golden decorations
  • Prosperity prayers
  • Special feeding
  • Community blessings

Kartik Purnima

Full Moon Blessings

Sacred full moon night dedicated to spiritual purification and cow blessings. Evening prayers under moonlight with special ceremonies.

Activities:

  • Moonlight prayers
  • Oil lamp ceremony
  • Special night feeding
  • Meditation sessions

Regular Community Programs

Ongoing activities that bring our community together in service and devotion

Daily Aarti & Prayers

Join us every morning and evening for beautiful cow aarti ceremonies. Experience the divine energy as we offer prayers, light incense, and sing devotional songs. Open to all devotees and families.

Program Details:

  • Schedule: Daily Morning 6:30 AM & Evening 6:00 PM
  • Duration: 30 minutes each session
  • Location: All three goshala centers
  • Participation: Open to all, no registration required

Educational Awareness Sessions

Weekly educational sessions about cow protection, sustainable agriculture, and spiritual significance of gau seva. Perfect for schools, colleges, and community groups.

Program Details:

  • Schedule: Every Saturday 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM
  • Age Group: All ages welcome (special programs for children)
  • Registration: Call us to book group sessions
  • Features: Interactive sessions with practical demonstrations

Volunteer Service Days

Monthly volunteer opportunities to serve our divine mother cows. Participate in feeding, cleaning, and caring activities while learning about cow protection.

Program Details:

  • Schedule: First Sunday of every month
  • Time: 8:00 AM - 12:00 PM
  • Activities: Feeding, cleaning, grooming, and healthcare
  • Registration: Required for planning purposes
